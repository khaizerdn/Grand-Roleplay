Available EntitySets:
- xmas


How To Set An EntitySet:
1) Open the MLO-Ymap with OpenIV or Codewalker.

2) Insert an Item inside "defaultEntitySets"

For Example:
<defaultEntitySets>
     <Item>xmas</Item>
</defaultEntitySets>
